💌 I Love You Meri Pyari Beti ❤️

Meri pyari si biwi,

Meri saanson ki wajah hai aap,
aur meri poori duniya hai aap.

Allah ka shukar hai ke usne mujhe
aap jaisi humsafar di.

Hamesha aise hi meri zindagi ka hissa rehna. ❤️
